ABOUT TheACE

TheACE is an E-Sports organization, formed by a group of college students of ITG in 2017, for college CSGO tournament.
Core members are Kartick, Nitesh, Shivang, Shubham.
Since then the bond has been unbreakable rather it has grown beautifully and now has become family of more than 75 people and counting.
Intent has always been to enjoy gaming and take break from everyday life.
